5 Fun Icebreaker Games for Your Next Hangout

Sometimes, getting a hangout started can be a bit awkward. Don't worry! These fun and simple games are perfect for breaking the ice and getting everyone laughing.

1. Two Truths and a Lie

Each person shares three statements about themselves: two are true, and one is a lie. The rest of the group has to guess which one is the lie. It’s a great way to learn surprising things about your friends!

2. Would You Rather?

Pose a dilemma to the group, like "Would you rather have a personal chef or a personal masseuse for life?" The answers often lead to hilarious discussions and debates.
